Tablet with sophisticated manufacturing process.

We want to create an affordable alernatives to expensive tablets like iPad, but we still put serious engineering thought into creating a body. The RecPad frame is the result of 13 carefully designed high-precision manufacturing processes. Using advanced engineering techniques and a keen artistic sense to ensure every last detail is technically and aesthetically perfect.

Powerful 1.8GHz Quad-Core Cortex A17 CPU

RecPad is built around 1.8GHz Quad-Core Cortex A17 CPU, which improves performance from 2.5 DMIPS/Mhz to 3.3 DMIPS/MHz than its predecessor, it is nearly 50% faster, in theory, compared to other CPUs when it comes to CPU performance.

ARM Mali-T764 GPU
Tablet with the 3D rendering capabilities of a desktop

With 400% the energy efficiency of the ARM Mali-T604 GPU, the ARM Mali-T760 GPU promises to deliver stunning graphics quality without compromising on power consumption. An advanced, coherent L2 Cache Interconnect ensures that the ARM Mali-T760 GPU can scale effectively to its full 16 cores, which enables the ARM Mali-T760 GPU to deliver images in games faster and more efficiently.

High color-saturation with intense, vivid colours

Same Retina display as iPad

RecPad uses the same Retina display as iPad, with a resolution of 2046 x 1536. We make the pixels under your fingertip more brilliant. Whether you’re looking at photos, watching videos, or using exciting apps, RecPad delivers vivid colors.

10-hour battery life

RecPad has an impressive 9500 mAh battery, that kept this tablet ticking for 10 hours, but it’s not the only reason to love your RecPad. This 10-inch tablet is only 7.5 mm thick and weighs only 1.19 lb.
