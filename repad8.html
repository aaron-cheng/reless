Small size with powerful heart

As the mini version of RecPad 10, RecPad 8 has exactly the same CPU and GPU. At 1.8GHz, Quad-Core Cortex A17 processor is the solution to your high CPU performance needs. That’s not all, thanks to the ARM Mali-T764 GPU and outstanding optimization at both the hardware and software level, now you can enjoy silky-smooth frame rates, whether you’re playing the newest 3D games or viewing HD images and videos.

 
 

7.9” IPS high resolution display

Games, e-books, and videos are more vibrant, clearer and bigger than ever on RecPad 8’s 7.9” fully laminated high resolution display with 2048x1536 resolution and 326 PPI, which delivers stunning color consistency from almost any angle.

Simplicity is hidden complexity.

RecPad 8 draws on nuanced thinking and precision artisanship to create the perfection of simplicity and beauty. Whether in software or hardware, we allow insightful design to speak for itself.
